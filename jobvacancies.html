Junior Graphic Designer

A fresh Graphic Designer is needed to join our team. You'll be designing a wide range of items for both digital and traditional media. You must be a self-starter, capable of providing fantastic creative ideas, and have exceptional attention to detail to be successful in this position. Your visuals should be eye-catching and convey the proper message to those who see them. You'll need a creative flare as well as a solid ability to translate requirements into design for this. We'd like to meet you if you can communicate well and work systematically as part of a team.
